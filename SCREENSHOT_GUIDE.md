### Screenshot 1: Obsidian
**File:** `obsidian-screenshot.png`

**How to capture:**
1. Open Obsidian on your computer
2. Press `Cmd/Ctrl + P` (Command Palette)
3. Type "graph" and select "Open graph view"
4. Make the window nice and wide
5. Take screenshot: `Cmd+Shift+4` (Mac) or `Windows+Shift+S` (Windows)
6. Save as: `obsidian-screenshot.png`
7. Move to: `/Users/nathan.baker/code/court_strategy/public/images/`

**What to show:**
- The graph view with connected notes
- Make it look cool and interconnected
- Shows the power of linked thinking
