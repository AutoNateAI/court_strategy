## Why Obsidian for Civic Research?

When dealing with legal proceedings, family planning, or community issues, you need to:
- Track complex, interconnected information
- Organize documents and evidence
- Build narratives and arguments
- Remember important dates and deadlines
- Connect facts and find patterns

Obsidian excels at all of these.

<Info>
  **Privacy First:** All your research stays on your computer. No cloud company can access your sensitive family or legal information.
</Info>

### Building Your Case Narrative

<Accordion title="How to connect your research">
  **Use bidirectional links:**
  ```markdown
  The custody agreement [[2020-Custody-Agreement]] shows that
  visitation was agreed upon. However, [[Text-Messages-2023]]
  demonstrate that access was denied. This is relevant to
  [[Key-Legal-Standard-Parental-Rights]].
  ```
  
  **Use tags for organization:**
  - `#evidence-strong` vs `#evidence-weak`
  - `#deadline-urgent`
  - `#question-for-attorney`
  - `#favorable` vs `#unfavorable`
  
  **Use Dataview to track:**
  ```dataview
  TABLE deadline, status
  FROM #case
  WHERE status != "complete"
  SORT deadline ASC
  ```
</Accordion>

### Daily Research Workflow

  <Step title="Morning Review">

### Search & Discovery

<Tip>
  **Pro search techniques:**
  - `tag:#evidence`: Find all evidence
  - `file:custody`: Find files with "custody"
  - `line:deadline`: Find lines mentioning deadlines
  - `path:Documents/`: Search specific folder
</Tip>

## Advanced: Using Canvas for Visual Planning

Canvas is perfect for:
- **Case Strategy:** Arrange arguments, evidence, and counterarguments visually
- **Family Planning:** Map out decision trees for school choices, etc.
- **Campaign Planning:** Visualize campaign strategy and tactics

<Accordion title="How to use Canvas">
  1. Create new note, add `.canvas` extension
  2. Drag notes, images, or PDFs onto canvas
  3. Connect items with arrows
  4. Color-code by category
  5. Export as image for presentations
</Accordion>

## Privacy & Security

<Warning>
  **Important security considerations:**
  - Enable encryption if syncing via cloud
  - Consider vault on encrypted drive for sensitive cases
  - Don't share vault with unauthorized people
  - Back up regularly but securely
  - Be careful with screenshots containing sensitive info
</Warning>

### Encryption Options

1. **File system encryption:** Use FileVault (Mac) or BitLocker (Windows)
2. **Encrypted folders:** Use VeraCrypt for vault folder
3. **Obsidian Sync:** Built-in end-to-end encryption
