## Community Guidelines

To keep our community helpful and welcoming:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Be Respectful">
    - Treat everyone with kindness
    - No judgment - we all start somewhere
    - Diverse perspectives strengthen us
    - Assume good intent
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Be Helpful">
    - Share what you've learned
    - Answer questions when you can
    - Provide constructive feedback
    - Celebrate others' wins
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Be Responsible">
    - Protect privacy (yours and others')
    - Don't share sensitive information
    - Credit sources and creators
    - Follow legal and ethical guidelines
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Be Patient">
    - Not everyone is technical
    - Questions aren't stupid
    - Learning takes time
    - Help is volunteer-based
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## How to Get Help

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search First">
    Check our [FAQ](/resources/faq) and guides - your question may be answered
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Context">
    When asking for help:
    - Describe what you're trying to do
    - Share what you've tried
    - Include error messages if any
    - Mention your OS (Mac/Windows/Linux)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Be Specific">
    "How do I link notes?" is better than "Obsidian isn't working"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Back">
    When you get help, share what worked for you
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently Asked Community Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is this community free?">
    Yes! Community participation is completely free. Some workshops may have fees, but community forums, resources, and office hours are free for everyone.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do I need to be technical?">
    Not at all! Our community welcomes people of all skill levels. Many members have zero technical background when they join.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I share my vault publicly?">
    You can share templates, structures, and approaches, but be very careful about sharing actual vault content that might contain personal or sensitive information. When in doubt, don't share.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How can I become a contributor?">
    Start by participating! Answer questions, share what you learn, and when you're ready, reach out about writing tutorials or contributing resources.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a code of conduct?">
    Yes, our community guidelines above serve as our code of conduct. We're building a respectful, helpful, and inclusive community.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
