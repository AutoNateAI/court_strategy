## Your Learning Path

Welcome to the self-paced learning track! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with Obsidian and Cursor for AI-powered research and planning.

## Learning Modules

### Module 1: Foundation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understanding the Tools">
    Learn what Obsidian and Cursor are, and why they're powerful together
    
    **Time:** 15 minutes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Installation">
    Install both tools on your computer
    
    **Resources:**
    - [Obsidian Installation Guide](/obsidian-guide/installation)
    - [Cursor Installation Guide](/cursor-guide/installation)
    
    **Time:** 20 minutes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Basic Setup">
    Configure your first Obsidian vault and connect Cursor
    
    **Time:** 30 minutes
  </Step>
</Steps>
