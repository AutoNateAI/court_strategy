## What to Expect

### Before the Workshop

<Checklist>
  - [ ] Install required software (we'll send instructions after registration)
  - [ ] Test your video and audio setup
  - [ ] Review any pre-workshop materials sent via email
  - [ ] Prepare questions you'd like to ask
</Checklist>

### During the Workshop

- **Interactive Learning:** Follow along on your own machine
- **Live Support:** Get help when you're stuck
- **Breakout Rooms:** Practice with peers in small groups
- **Screen Sharing:** See exactly how things work
- **Recording Available:** Can't make it live? You'll get access to the recording

### After the Workshop

- **Lifetime Access:** Keep all materials and recordings
- **Community Support:** Join our forum for ongoing help
- **Office Hours:** Attend weekly follow-up sessions
- **Certificate:** Receive a completion certificate
