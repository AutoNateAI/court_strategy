## Welcome to AutoNateAI Workshops

Our workshops are designed to help families, researchers, and community members master AI-powered tools for research, planning, and organization. Whether you're completely new to these tools or looking to level up your skills, we have something for you.

## Prerequisites

<Note>
  **No coding experience required!** These workshops are designed for everyone, regardless of technical background.
</Note>

You will need:
- A computer (Mac, Windows, or Linux)
- Internet connection
- Willingness to learn
- Optional: A notebook for taking traditional notes
